It's Peanut Butter Jelly Time!

The Path to PBJ Perfection

How do we get these two cuties together? Follow these easy steps!
You'll need: two slices of bread, a knife, a jar of jelly, a jar of peanut butter, and a can-do attitude!

  1. Use your knife to carefully slather jelly onto one side of one slice of your bread. Really go for it.
  2. Use your knife to slather a generous amount of peanut butter onto one side of your other slice of bread. Careful with your PB to J ratio here - no one likes a soggy sandwich.
  3. Gently and lovingly take your two slices of bread and put them together, being sure that the jelly and peanut butter are now joined in perfect harmony.
